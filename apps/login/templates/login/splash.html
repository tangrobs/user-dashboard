Welcome to Dashr

On Dashr, you can visit your friends' pages, send and receive messages, and leave comments to engage in deep and philosophical conversations... or just talk about cats and programming. It's up to you!

Manage users

As a Dashr user, you can see all the other users' public information and leave them messages. As an admin, you'll also be able to alter the users' attributes.

Leave messages

Any user can leave a message for any other user, and even comment on previously left messages.

Edit user information

Admins have the ability to edit other users' information, such as their name and email address.
